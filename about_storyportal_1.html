Story Portal 1

This is a choose-your-own-adventure style story
that takes place in an apocalpytic Seattle.


We originally showed this project at Art Neureau 2023, a neuroscience themed art show in Seattle.
Inspired by choose-your-own adventure books,
online personality tests,
and neural circuits -
we wanted to create a multimedia project that is greater than the sum of its parts.
The website is constructed of linked web pages
with connected paths informed by neural circuit motifs,
e.g. recurrence, parallel pathways, feedforward/back.
