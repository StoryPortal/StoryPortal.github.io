As the house music plays on, you look out at the window at Elliott Bay and notice the waves becoming more and more regular.

The waves crash with the beat of the music!

The water starts to part and you notice a black hole form.

You notice a big cloud of colorful butterflies move in a cloud across the water towards the witch’s nest.

The butterflies encircle the nest and one of them says to you,

“You’ve gotta get out of here!! Some of us will distract her and you can come with us!”

You let the butterflies envelop you and take you away in their cloud
